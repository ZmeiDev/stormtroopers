About Stormtroopers

THE IDEA

  • Stormtroopers is a web application inspired by the need for merchandise that is created by star wars fans. Our users/stormtroopers are these who create and decide what drawings to be used for the various products offered by us. Can you imagine a t-shirt or a mug with the drawing you have created? Yes, that’s for real and it’s not so difficult to do it! All you need to do is to upload your drawing and wait for other users to rate it. At the end of every week the top 3 most rated drawings get printed on all types of products we offer and become available for purchase to everyone. Isn’t that cool? :)

  • Don’t be shy! Roll you sleeves up and start creating the next fan inspired t-shirt. If you don’t have any ideas now, you always can browse through the already uploaded drawings and the products we have created with them. And don’t forget to rate other troopers’ drawings, so the wheel can keep rolling.
